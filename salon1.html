With over 28 years of experience, we have confidence in making you
a Totally Nu Yu.

Hair-styling is a collaborative effort. Not only will we share our
expertise to bring your hairstyle vision to life but also our knowledge
so you can create & maintain it on your own. We've got you covered
from fades to foils and specialize in colouring, permanents and
Asian hairstyling. As locals to the immensely diverse Hamilton,
we embrace all. If it helps, we can fluently communicate in
Chinese (Cantonese & Mandarin), Vietnamese, Italian and Portuguese.
